About SMUN'25

The first Tripoli-wide MUN conference bringing together students from across Libya

Welcome to SMUN'25

South Mediterranean University Model United Nations '25 is the first Tripoli-wide MUN conference bringing together students from across Libya. This pioneering event marks a significant milestone for the Libyan MUN community as the first conference of its scale in the capital city.

Our conference will feature diverse committees addressing critical global issues, welcoming delegates who demonstrate English proficiency and genuine passion for international affairs. With over 200 expected participants from across Libya, SMUN'25 promises to be a transformative experience that sets a new standard for Model UN in the country.

Join us for three days of intensive debate, diplomatic negotiation, and academic excellence as we work together to address the world's most pressing challenges while building bridges across Libya's educational community.
