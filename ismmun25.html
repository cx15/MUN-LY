Conference Overview

ISMMUN'25 exceeded expectations with expanded participation and outstanding diplomatic achievements within the ISM community

A Remarkable Success

ISMMUN'25 marked a significant milestone for Model United Nations at the International School of Martyrs, building upon the success of the inaugural ISMMUN'24. The conference brought together 80 participants from the ISM community, with 57 students serving as delegates across two specialized committees.

Over three intensive days, delegates engaged in high-level diplomatic discussions in ECOSOC and UNODC, addressing critical global issues with increased complexity and academic rigor. The conference featured enhanced formats, deeper committee work, and expanded networking opportunities that strengthened the ISM MUN community.

The success of ISMMUN'25 has solidified ISM's commitment to diplomatic education, setting higher standards for academic excellence and demonstrating the school's growing expertise in Model United Nations.
