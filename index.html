Empowering Libya's Future Diplomats

MUNLY is Libya's premier platform for Model United Nations conferences, connecting students, educators, and institutions across the country.

Since our inception, we've been dedicated to fostering diplomatic education, critical thinking, and global awareness among Libyan youth. Our platform serves as the central hub for MUN activities, providing comprehensive information about conferences, resources for participants, and tools for organizers.
